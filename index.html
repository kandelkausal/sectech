    How to play

    1. Only level 1 is open at first. Finish it to unlock the next.
    2. Read the row of tiles and work out what goes in the gap.
    3. Type your answer in the box, then press Submit or Enter.
    4. Wrong guess? Read the message and try again.
    5. After each level you’ll see the pattern card you earned — tap Next to continue.

    Pattern cards

    You earn a card when you finish a level. Locked cards show “???” until then.
